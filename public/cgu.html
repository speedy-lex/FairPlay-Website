Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is FairPlay truly free?

A: Yes, FairPlay is 100% free and ad-free. There are no subscription fees, hidden costs, or premium features. Our project is supported by community contributions and voluntary donations.

Q: How is FairPlay open-source and community-driven?

A: The entire FairPlay codebase is available on GitHub under the MIT License. This means anyone can view, contribute to, and help improve the platform. Our development is guided by community feedback and contributions from developers, designers, translators, and other enthusiasts.

Q: How does FairPlay protect my privacy?

A: We are deeply committed to privacy. We collect minimal data, primarily for account management (email, pseudo) and aggregated, anonymous usage statistics. We do not sell your data, track your personal viewing habits for advertising, or engage in any data monetization practices.

Q: What kind of content is allowed on FairPlay?

A: FairPlay focuses on relevant and entertaining content. We strictly prohibit misinformation, political discussions, and religious content to maintain a neutral and enjoyable environment. Please refer to our Code of Conduct for detailed guidelines.

Q: Can I contribute to FairPlay?

A: Absolutely! We welcome contributions from everyone, regardless of technical skill. You can contribute by writing code, translating, creating documentation, designing UI/UX, moderating content, reporting bugs, or simply by spreading the word. Visit our Contributors Guide for more information.

Q: Where can I report bugs or issues?

A: You can report bugs or issues via our GitHub issue tracker, or our discord server https://discord.gg/AZBwM6u9Kr. Please provide as much detail as possible to help us reproduce and fix the problem.
